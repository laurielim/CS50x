About Me

Hi, my name is Laurie Lim. I was born and raised in Mauritius. I love solving problems and coming up with neat solutions.

At 19 years old, I moved to Barcelona, Spain to study at ESADE Business School. I graduated with a bachelor’s degree in business administration in 2018. Since then, I have been working for my family’s business, a manufacturing company of over 100 employees. I worked alongside the following departments: sales, marketing, operations and human resource to improve our processes. Additionally, I filled the gaps whenever it was necessary to ensure the smooth running of company activities.

During my last semester of business school, I interned at Yumm!, a start-up which provides restaurant menus and information in various languages via a mobile application. After graduating, I worked there for an additional 4 months before moving back to Mauritius. Working at Yumm! gave me my first experience with mobile app development and programming. I learnt about strings, quality assurance, and the use of tools like JIRA and Confluence. I also processed a lot of data, and discovered the power of scripting. I was blown away by what could be accomplished by a few lines of code.
